Economic Potential for Hydrogen

By Mark F. Ruth, Paige Jadun, Nicholas Gilroy, Elizabeth Connelly, Richard Boardman, A.J. Simon, Amgad Elgowainy, and Jarett Zuboy

OBTAINING DATA
The data files can be obtained from the National Renewable Energy Laboratory via the Internet from OpenEI.

GENERAL
The data are provided in a comma-separated values (CSV) file.

DATA FILES
Hydrogen_Economic_Potential_Supply_Demand: A dataset that provides estimates on hydrogen’s economic potential as estimated in H2@Scale scenarios.

ATTRIBUTES
Attribute Label: gid
Attribute Definition: Unique identifier.

Attribute Label: scenario
Attribute Definition: Names of scenario corresponding to H2@Scale economic potential scenarios.

Attribute Label: latitude
Attribute Definition: Latitude of supply or demand location.

Attribute Label: longitude
Attribute Definition: Longitude of supply or demand location.

Attribute Label: demand_ammonia_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for ammonia production in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_ldv_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for light-duty vehicles in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_metals_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for metals refining in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_methanol_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for methanol production in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_mhdv_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for medium- and heavy-duty vehicles in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_ng_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for supplementing natural gas in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_refining_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for oil refining in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_storage_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for seasonal energy storage for the electric grid in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: demand_synfuel_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential demand for synthetic fuel production in kilograms of hydrogen.


Attribute Label: supply_lte_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential supply from low-temperature electroysis production in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: supply_nuclear_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential supply from high-temperature electroysis production using nuclear-generated heat in kilograms of hydrogen.

Attribute Label: supply_smr_kg
Attribute Definition: Hydrogen’s economic potential supply from steam methane reforming production in kilograms of hydrogen.
